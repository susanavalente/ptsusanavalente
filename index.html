Who is Susana? Why am I doing this?

My name is Susana and I am from Lisbon, Portugal. Since I can remember, my dream was to be a Personal Trainer and help other people to achieve their goals and dreams. When I was 18 years old, I became a student of one the most prestigious european schools in Health in Exercise - Faculty of Human Kinetics (Lisbon).

Between 2007 and 2010, I graduated in Health and Exercise and then, between 2010 and 2012 I became a Master in this field. Since then I help people to achieve their fitness and emotional goals! And I am the happiest person in the world to have the opportunity to do what I love every single day! My specialization is rehabilitation and working with elderly people. Nevertheless, my days are almost about weight loss programs and hypertrophy programs.

At the moment, I am a Personal Trainer, a group fitness instructor, a school coordinator and university professor (Faculty of Human Kinetics).
