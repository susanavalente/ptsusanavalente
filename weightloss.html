What is a Weight Loss Program?

A Weight Loss Program is mostly about 70% nutrition and 30% working out. For this program it is mandatory to have a nutricionist working with you! If you don't know how to eat and what each nutrient means to your body, this can be super difficult and super challenging. In this program, you will understand how calories can be your best friend In this program you will feel an intensity raise every week.

This program has the duration of 3 months. The main goal of this program is to help you to understanding the basics of nutrition and working out, so you can apply the knowledge in your daily life.
